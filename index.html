The Viral Voice

(First time in LPU history a E-singing show)

The viral voice is a E-singing show which is being organized by student organization Factum on the National level. We are giving a chance to the talent to compete across the nation and win great prizes.

It is the platform on which anyone can participate. We believe that talent cannot be measured by the age/language/region etc.

What Is Process ?

1
REGISTRATION

Register Simply Now by Paying 50 Rupees.

2
AUDITION

Register and submit the recorded video for audition. Video will be uploaded on our social media and top 70% will be selected out of 70% top 50% for Round-1 and rest 20% for Wildcard Entry on the bases of online voting on your audition video.

3
ROUND-1

These 50% will compete for round-2 and top 20% will be selected for the round-2 by our judges.

4
WILDCART ENTRY

20% of Audition round will get a chance in wildcard round they need to send their video us that will be uploaded on our YouTube channel and after the voting of 24 hours best 2-4% will get a chance to directly perform in the Round-2.

5
ROUND-2

These 20% and 2-4% of wildcard participants will perform in this round and judging criteria for this round is 50-50. 50% points by judges and 50% by audience after this round top 8% will be selected.

6
FINALS

Top 8% will perform in the finals and winner will be selected by judges.
